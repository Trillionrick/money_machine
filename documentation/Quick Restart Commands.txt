 Quick Restart Commands

  Method 1: Clean Restart (Recommended)

  # Kill old processes and restart
  lsof -ti:8080 | xargs kill -9 2>/dev/null; sleep 2 && ./start_dashboard.sh

  Method 2: Step-by-Step

  # Step 1: Kill the dashboard
  lsof -ti:8080 | xargs kill -9

  # Step 2: Wait a moment
  sleep 2

  # Step 3: Start fresh
  ./start_dashboard.sh

  Method 3: Nuclear Option (if port still stuck)

  # Kill everything related to the dashboard
  pkill -9 -f "uvicorn|web_server"
  lsof -ti:8080 | xargs kill -9
  sleep 3
  ./start_dashboard.sh
